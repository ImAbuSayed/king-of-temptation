[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook 1]
Main takda haan teri akh dangerous, sohniye
Hanere vich feel karda haan tera nasha
Main har pal karda haan chase your love
Mere sahan vich hundi ae blend teri mehek

[Hook 2]
Main bolda haan gall sachi from my heart
Tu hasdi ae looking so fine kudiye
Main turda haan nede crossing every line
Tainu pauna ae main tonight har haal

[Verse 1]
Kaale asmaan ch chamke bright teri tor
Main labhda haan tera shadow shehar vich
Thandi hawa vich low key chalde mere saah
Main lukanwa haan gham apne in a glass
Puraane raste te aundi flash teri yaad
Main chhad ditta hun wasting my time
Main jitna ae sab kuch crossing the sky
Main karda haan vaada making you mine

[Pre-Chorus]
Doori teri kardi ae heart mera weak
Main mangda haan rab ton just one chance
Khamoshi vich sunda haan loud teri awaaz
Main udna ae tere naal far away

[Chorus]
Main karda haan tera nasha crazy for you
Tu lutt laye mere chain taking my mind
Main jeyonda haan tere layi all day long
Mere seene vich dhadke tu beating so strong
Main rakhda haan tainu safe mere khwab vich
Teri nigaah ch vasse pure magic asar
Main dinda haan apni jaan for your smile
Tu aaja mere nede stay for a while

[Verse 2]
Main ghumaanda haan tatte tyre burning the road
Mere yaar saare pakke keeping the code
Main jhalda haan nakhre toxic and cold
Tere karke main badleya making me bold
Par tere aage karda main drop mera guard
Tu kitti ae hoshiyari stealing my heart
Main larda haan duniya naal breaking the rules
Main ban gaya haan aashiq playing the fool
